How It Works
📋
5 Report Types Supported
Incident, Arrest, Use of Force, Traffic Accident, and Supplemental reports. Each type loads type-specific fields and smart checklists automatically.
✏️
Guided + Free-Form Input
Use the structured form for step-by-step guided entry, or switch to free-form mode to paste or dictate raw notes — ReportShield structures it either way.
⚡
Smart Checklist System
Per-report-type dynamic prompt chips remind officers of critical details before generating. Miranda read? Body cam activated? Evidence collected?
🤖
AI Narrative Generation
Powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. Produces a professional, third-person, past-tense law enforcement narrative using only the facts the officer provides. No speculation. No fabrication.
🔍
Built-In Audit Trail
Every action is timestamped and logged: session start, generation events, officer edits, copy actions, and final submission. Full chain-of-custody for report writing.
✅
Officer Verification Checkpoint
Before submission, officers must check off five mandatory review items confirming accuracy, factual integrity, and personal accountability.
⚠️
AI Disclosure Notice
Every generated narrative is accompanied by a mandatory disclosure banner. The AI's role is transparent and the officer's certification is required.
💻
Hardware-Agnostic
Runs in any modern browser. No app install, no proprietary hardware, no vendor lock-in. Works on department computers, MDTs, and personal devices.
