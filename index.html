How It Works

5 Report Types Supported

Incident, Arrest, Use of Force, Traffic Accident, and Supplemental reports. Each type loads type-specific fields and smart checklists automatically.

Guided + Free-Form Input

Officers can use the structured form mode for step-by-step guided entry, or switch to free-form mode to paste or dictate raw notes — ReportShield structures it either way.

Smart Checklist System

Per-report-type dynamic prompt chips remind officers of critical details before generating: Miranda read? Body cam activated? Evidence collected? Answered items are marked with a green checkmark.

AI Narrative Generation

Powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. Produces a professional, third-person, past-tense law enforcement narrative using only the facts the officer provides. No speculation. No fabrication. Court-ready output.

Built-In Audit Trail

Every action is timestamped and logged: session start, generation events, officer edits, copy actions, and final submission. Full chain-of-custody for the report writing process.

Officer Verification Checkpoint

Before submission, officers must check off five mandatory review items confirming accuracy, factual integrity, and personal accountability. The submit button only activates when all boxes are cleared.

AI Disclosure Notice

Every generated narrative is accompanied by a mandatory disclosure banner: the AI's role is transparent, and the officer's review and certification is required before submission.

Hardware-Agnostic

Runs in any modern browser. No app install, no proprietary hardware, no vendor lock-in. Works on department computers, MDTs, and personal devices.
